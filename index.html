Quality Cooking Party Guidelines
My friends have enjoyed and endured a number of cooking parties. Here is what we have learned from them:
  1. Prerequisites
    • Plan for the cooking
    • Complete set of ingredients
    • Fresh ingredients
    • Appropriate drinks
    • Enough energy
    • More than enough time
  2. Environment variables
    • Good company
    • Thematic (to the dish) music
    • Proper lighting
  3. Actual cooking
    • Pour the drinks
    • Begin the prepared plan
    • Distribute the tasks accordingly
    • Communicate
    • Taste while cooking
    • Dynamically adapt the plan
  4. Serving
    • Discuss the presentation
    • Get some serving ideas from everybody
    • Arrange the plates beautifully
    • Arrange the table like a masterpiece
    • Enjoy the taste (do not hurry)

Straight Razor Shaving

Let me explain:

  • First of all, it is less dangerous than driving.
  • For some people, it is a hobby like any other. They collect and experiment with different shaving gear.
  • Others point out that disposable blades are eco-unfriendly.
  • If you do it right, the shave with a straight razor is closer, compared to the shave with a disposable blade.
  • Straight razor shaving requires some initial capital, but can be viewed as an investment, for a razor's useful life is more than a hundred years.
  • With a straight razor, shaving becomes a pleasant morning ritual.
